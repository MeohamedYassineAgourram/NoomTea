About Us

Tucked away in the vibrant Saint-Germain neighborhood, Noom Tea is more than just a bubble tea shop - it's a cozy, creative space where flavor, culture, and community meet. Every drink is made to order, with fresh ingredients, customizable sweetness, and dairy-free options to match your mood. Whether you're a fan of creamy matcha, brown sugar milk tea, or refreshing fruit blends, you'll find your favorite here. Step inside and you’ll discover a warm, welcoming vibe - complete with wood accents, soft lighting, and even a piano and bookshelf to relax with. Noom Tea is where regulars come for their daily boba fix and new visitors leave with a smile.
